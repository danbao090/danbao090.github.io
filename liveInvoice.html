Live Invoice

Live invoice is a personalized invoice that can be sent out via email, SMS or linked to the company app/MyPages. The content can be personalized based on the recipient's behavior and preference. User can pay an invoice directly via a chosen payment method.
In this project, I worked as a UI/UX designer in an agile team consisting of developers, product owner and designers.
My main tasks were developing the concept, defining user flow, conducting user research and designing style guide & user interfaces.
