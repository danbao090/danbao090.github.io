Evry young professional illustration

Evry Young Professionals (YP) is a network primarily for those in EVRY who feel under 35 years old.
YP works to reach these goals through organizing academic and social events based on feedback from members, various company disciplines and topical themes.
Examples of events we organise and are involved in:
- Academic events with topics such as inspiration, internal mobility, innovation etc. These events typically consist of lectures, workshops, dinner and socialising.
- Seminars
- Assisting HR on stands at universities and colleges
- Contribution to internal projects and events (ambassadors, OD-day etc.)
- Summer Party


In this project, I worked as a UI/UX designer in an agile team consisting of developers, project managers, testers and  designers.
My main tasks were developing concept, defining user flow, designing style guide & user interfaces.
