Evry Young Professionals Illustration

Evry Young Professionals (YP) is a network primarily for those in EVRY who feel under 35 years old.
YP organizes academic and social events based on feedback from members, various company disciplines and topical themes. Examples of events we organise and are involved in:
- Academic events with topics such as inspiration, internal mobility, innovation etc. These events typically consist of lectures, workshops, dinner and socialising.
- Assisting HR on stands at universities and colleges
- Contribution to internal projects and events
- Summer Party

I made illustrations for both of the YP groups in Bergen and Oslo. They were used for online activities in YP groups.
