Cash Pool

Cash Pool aims to provide a service that (for one customer) brings balances from different accounts together in a net value that will be used for interest calculations. The main purpose for this product is to achieve interest netting and better utilization of available liquidity.

In the migration from the JSF client to Angular and with the new requirements for Cash Pool in regards to WCAG 2.0 & WCAG 2.1 we have decided to reshape the interfaces and functionalites in different sub pages. The main goal is to improve the user experience and become compliant with the new regulatory requirements.

I worked as a UI/UX designer in an agile team consisting of developers, project managers, testers and designers.
